The second volume of "My Letters" continues the journey begun in the first book, gathering over 350 selected letters published mainly in the Readers' Forum of O Estado de S. Paulo newspaper. Written by an author who at 86 years old continues committed to public debate, these letters represent thoughts and dreams of a life dedicated to Brazil.

Organization by Period

2009

Beginning of the second volume, marking the continuity of reflections. Letters about Brazil's challenges in the post-2008 global financial crisis scenario.

2010-2014

Period of major events in Brazil: preparation for World Cup and Olympics, 2013 protests, debates about the country's political and economic direction.

2015-2019

Years of political turbulence, economic crisis, and social transformations. Reflections on ethics, corruption, institutions, and the future of Brazilian democracy.

2020-2025

COVID-19 pandemic, its impacts on society, and contemporary challenges. Letters that conclude more than six decades of civic participation.

About the Publication

The over 350 letters in this volume were selected from among the many published over 16 years in the Readers' Forum of O Estado de S. Paulo newspaper and in other Brazilian newspapers. Each letter represents a moment of reflection on events in Brazil and the world.

This volume is dedicated to the author's family: to Vera, beloved wife and companion of 58 years, to children Alexandre, Luciana, and Cristina, and to grandchildren Luiza, Rodrigo, Guilherme, Vinícius, and Nathalia — "who are the fulfillment of my life."
