My Letters

expressions of an idealist

1958 — 2008

Volume 1

The first volume of "My Letters" gathers fifty years of reflections, opinions, and ideals published in various newspapers and magazines. From the first letters written during the years of study in the United States to the beginning of the new millennium, this volume documents the evolution of a Brazilian's thinking who always believed in the transformative power of public dialogue.

ISBN: 978-65-01-56953-6
Publisher: Author's Edition, 2025

Organization by Period

The Beginning (1958-1962)

Letters written during the years of study in Pittsburgh, USA. Includes correspondence with president-elect John F. Kennedy, the Governor of Pennsylvania, and publications in the Pittsburgh Press and Time magazine.

The 1980s

Beginning of regular publications in O Estado de S. Paulo newspaper. Reflections on Brazil during the redemocratization period, economy, and social transformations. Letters from 1983, 1985, 1986, 1987, 1988, and 1989.

The 1990s

Consolidation as a Readers' Forum contributor. Comments on the Collor, Itamar, and FHC governments, the Real Plan, and Brazil's transformations on the world stage. Years 1991 to 1999.

21st Century (2001-2008)

Reflections on the new millennium, globalization, national and international politics. Includes letters from brother Marcelo on related topics. Continuation of the commitment to public debate.
