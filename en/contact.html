A Message from the Author

"Many ideas and many dreams are presented in the letters of this book. All published and read by thousands of readers. Were they worth it? Will their proposals and suggestions one day bear fruit? To me it doesn't matter. The seeds have been sown. Let us keep dreaming and fighting, for at the right moment and with help from Above, our dreams may become reality."
— Silvano Correa
